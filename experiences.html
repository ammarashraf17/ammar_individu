ALL FOR THE LOVE OF MONEY

Nur Ammar, Jan 13, 2020

In the last few days after the Newtown Connecticut shooting, I was really saddened, like many people around the world have. Another needless assassination, and this time not just involving innocent people but tiny, innocent children, by another mentally ill person who had just had free access to weapons of high power.

I believe it's the person pulling the trigger who kills and not the gun, because I have share with a previous post "Guard Your Mind." But here and now I am not suggesting that I am in support of the ready market for high-powered assault weapons. It has nothing whatsoever with the 2nd modification and all things to do with those who live off this ignorance and who do not care more than themselves.

The NRA and Gun Owners Of America, headed by Larry Pratt, are very well sponsored for launching their campaigns. There's plenty of money that can change hands through weapons sales. You claim that in lame efforts to persuade you that you would forfeit this right if some form of weapon is outlawed, you can stand for your 2nd amendment rights. It's a complete lie, and its employers are the only ones that will be harmed. They are all willing to give your life for love of money!

It is a shame that many of the actions that are made are due to this love of money or not to the best interests that some people would like you to believe. For example take planned obsolescence: there is a vast majority of goods intentionally designed NOT for the long term, so that you have to purchase them again once used, which might otherwise be made to last. The claim here is to build and sustain jobs, where are they? The FDA closes eyes on toxic genetically modified food, which is primarily responsible for the issue of obesity and diseases connected to other health problems in the United States, but since these crops are well paid for, they do so once again without your best interests. Anything for money love! The list can keep running!
