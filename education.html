MY EDUCATION

Kindergarten

My first kindergarten began at Tadika Ilmiah Bestari, where I learned to write, read, calculate and dance. And that was one time when I was introduced with Indian and Chinese friends to a multicultural tradition. Unfortunately, because of my nomadic condition, I do not remember enough of the place since I was to young to be remember much.

Primary School

I studied at SK Puncak Alam 2 during my primary school. Since I was friends with all my classmates, I have a lot of sweet memories about the place. It was also the time to play and think about it with little difficulty.

In 2011, I was moved from my house to SK Puncak Alam 3. I was studying there and my academic success was fantastic and I was a head prefect. Just two years I studied there.

Secondary School

I've been admitted as a student at SMK Puncak Alam and been a fantastic school student. Initially, my life is complicated to adopt. I adapt to the situation over the years and improve to become a stronger, independent person. I applied to be the school prefect during this time but rejected it because I would like to concentrate on academics and not need many responsibilities.

University

And I'm now a proud UITM Rembau student at the Information Management Faculty. I'm already in my last six-month period and I am set to end my last six-month period in January 2021.
